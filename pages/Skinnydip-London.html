PURPOSE

Skinnydip first turned to us in 2016, needing a full redesign and rebuild. Fast‑forward a few years and it was time for a refresh. We consolidated their offering to a single multi‑currency store that thrives on our custom speedy framework. Since then, we've carried out a number of strategic projects, including a site takeover for their limited edition Barbie collection, a custom filter rebuild that nods to their product breadth, and a killer BFCM strategy (to name a few).
